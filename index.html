Fairytale characters are real and walk among us.

In The Wolf Among Us 2, step into the role of Bigby, also known as "The Big Bad Wolf" and the Sheriff of Fabletown. Dive back into a dark, noir world of detective work where happy endings are nowhere to be found. Set six months after the first season, the game unfolds in winter-stricken New York City. A fresh case emerges, blurring the boundaries between Fabletown and the NYPD. Your decisions in handling this case may shape the fate of the Fable community. The Wolf Among Us series by Telltale Games draws inspiration from the Fables comics published by DC Comics.
